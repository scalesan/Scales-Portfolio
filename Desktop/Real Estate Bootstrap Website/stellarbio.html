Stellar Realty

With over 30 years of combined Real Estate and Finance experience, the team at the Stellar Realty Group has the knowledge and expertise to navigate Homeowners, Investors, Investment Groups and Condominium Associations through the wonderful world of Michigan Real Estate. The Stellar Realty Group houses 2 Real Estate Brokerages, a full service Property Management company as well as a full service Real Estate Brokerage.

Both companies are small enough to continue to provide personalized service, but large enough to handle your most complexed Real Estate transactions.

We specialize in performing real estate and property management transactions in Wayne, Oakland, Macomb, Washtenaw, and Genesee counties.

Our portfolio consists of approximately 400 SFR, Multi- units, and Condos.
